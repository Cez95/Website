About Me

My name is Christopher Olson and I am an MBA student MBA with a focus in information systems at the University of New Hampshire. I have a strong passion for application development. I currently know Swift3, Python and Firebase really well, however, I have experience using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Objective C, and Java. When I obtain my MBA, I hope to work in the tech. industry. My long-term goal is to start my own software company.

As a college freshman, I was an Athletic Training major. I chose Athletic Training because I was fascinated with the human body and I really wanted to help people. Unfortunately, it was not the right avenue for me. I realized that there was more that I could do to help people with my work. After working hard to bring up my grades, I was accepted into the Peter T. Paul College of Business and Economics at the University of New Hampshire. This was a fantastic experience because I learned an incredible amount about entrepreneurship and became exposed to people who have become close friends.

My hobbies include mountain biking, surfing, working out, watching movies, and playing video games. When I was a freshman in college I also pledged Alpha Sigma Phi fraternity. This was a great experience because it opened a huge network of close friends I still remain in contact with. I have also competed in a physique competition which I had been training towards for over a year.

Currently I am balancing a full-time MBA schedule while developing an iOS application that I have been working on for the past two months. I love learning new things and I plan to hone my development skills for the rest of my life.
